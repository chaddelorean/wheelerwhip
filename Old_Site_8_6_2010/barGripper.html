Bar Gripper




  • Clamps to 3/4" - 7/8" round bar
  • Colors: Silver
  • Made from high grade aluminum.
  • Easy to install!
  • Simple to use and durable.
    •
 

This flag mount is designed to clamp on the verticle bars of an 3/4" - 7/8" round bar and comes with a 1/2" hole for mounting your desired Wheelerwhip safety whip flag.

When installing, find a good place to install your flag on any verticle 3/4" - 7/8" bar. Unbolt the mount and reassemble it around the the bar. Tighten the bolts firmly. Install your Wheelerwhip quick release flag mount or your bolt flag, and your ready to go. (Allen head wrench is included for mounting)
