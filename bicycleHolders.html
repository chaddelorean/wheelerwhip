Bicycle Bracket


  • Works with any 1/4" - 7/16" bolt
  • Colors: Silver
    •
  • Simple and Easy to install.
  • Holds 1/4" diameter flag poles only.
  • Mounts to any bicycle rear axle, or any bolt 7/16 of an inch or smaller.
  • Flag wedges firmly into bracket.
  • Size is approximately 5 1/2" in length and 1" in width.
    •
 

Steel Bracket


   

Installation: Loosen the rear axle nut on the side of your bicycle. Slide the bracket into place. Adjust the bracket to your desired angle, and tighten the axle nut firmly. This will work the same on any bolt that is 1/4 - 7/16 of an inch.
